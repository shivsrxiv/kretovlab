## Principal Investigator

**Dr. Dmitry Kretov**  
Assistant Professor at Université Laval and Group Leader in the Oncology Division at the CHU de Québec–Université Laval Research Center. The lab studies how RNA-binding proteins and microRNAs regulate mRNA stability and translation in development and disease, and develops tools such as **RBPscan** to analyze RNA–protein interactions in vivo.
